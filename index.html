ABOUT US

Rom & Co is small and unique cafe located in Whitfords Shopping Centre. It is popular place for weekdays and weekend breakfast and lunch. We offer a great range of specialty coffee drinks as well as breakfast and lunch dishes like sandwiches and salads. We  also offer a good choice of gluten free and dairy free produce with vegan options. In addition, we also have a healthy range options.

We aim to provide friendly, effecient and quality service and food to our customers. There is something for everyone at Rom & Co. our number one goal is to provide quality food and service to our valuable customers.

Please explore our menu as we have plenty of options to pleasure everyone.
